Indian Institute of Management, Ranchi

Indian Institute of Management Ranchi (IIM Ranchi) was the ninth IIM established in India of all the 20 IIMs. Established in 2010, it offers numerous courses in the field of Management at postgraduate, doctoral and certificate level such as PGP, PGPHRM, PhD, CPGM among others.
